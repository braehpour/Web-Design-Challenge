Summary: Latitude vs. X

In this project we were tasked with locating at least 500 cities from around the world and find how various aspects of the weather change they approach the equator. This was accomplished by pulling data in from the OpenWeatherMap API.

Once the data was collected, we used Matplotlib to create several visualizations that compared the given aspect of weather to the city's latitude.

The factors I chose to examine were Latitude:

  • vs Maximum Temperature
  • vs Cloudiness
  • vs Humidity
  • vs Wind Speed
